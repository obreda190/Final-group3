1.) Players will 'buzz in' by using pre-determined key-binds
Player 1's buzzer is the 'Q' key
Player 2's buzzer is the 'B' key
Player 3's buzzer is the 'P' key
2.) Each question will only be asked once, there is no chance to steal
3.) The names of the categories will sometimes give a hint as to what sort of answer is expected
ex. Category name: Ends in "X", Every answer will be a word that ends in the letter X
4.) Answers are NOT case-sensitive, but they ARE punctuation-sensitive (i.e. apostrophes and hyphens are important)
5.) The last person to answer a question correctly gets to choose the next question
